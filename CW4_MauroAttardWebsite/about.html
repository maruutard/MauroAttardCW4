About

A freelance photographer based in Malta focusing on portraiture, food, couples and documentary photography. Inspired by other artist's works and research allows my work to evolve and improve. Therefore, I am thrilled that by using photography I am able to preserve the moments that I experience in my life. I come from a small island in the Mediterranean called, Malta, in which I was born and raised. I aspire to travel the world, meet new people and make memories.
